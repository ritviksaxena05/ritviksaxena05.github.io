Design Wizard!

UI Designer / Web Developer
Roux Institute, Northeastern Univeristy

For my role as a Design Wizard at Roux Institute, Northeastern University, I was deeply engaged in bringing innovative UI/UX solutions to life for a range of startup clients. Over a six-month period, I collaborated with nearly 15 diverse startups to support them in building user-centric digital products. My responsibilities extended to managing end-to-end design workflows, including branding, pitch deck creation, and crafting intuitive user interfaces.
I successfully executed over 28 projects, leveraging tools like Figma, Adobe Creative Suite, and wireframing techniques to deliver polished design solutions. Each project was an opportunity to drive creativity and provide hands-on design support during critical scaling and growth phases, enhancing each startup’s visual identity and user engagement. This experience not only honed my design skills but also strengthened my ability to work collaboratively across interdisciplinary teams to meet high-impact goals.

HomeBro!

HomeBro – Home Decor Shopping and Consultation App

For HomeBro, I designed an extensive user experience with over 45 unique screens in Figma, aimed at making home decor shopping and consultation accessible and engaging. The app allows users to browse a curated collection of home decor items, seek expert consultations, and customize their shopping experience to match their style preferences. With a clean and user-friendly interface, the app simplifies the journey from inspiration to purchase. Every screen was crafted to be intuitive, ensuring seamless navigation and visual appeal, making HomeBro a one-stop solution for all home decor needs.

PickSpot!

PickSpot – Parking Spot Booking App

For PickSpot, I designed an intuitive design with over 25 screens on Figma to create a seamless parking solution for urban drivers. This app allows users to easily search for and reserve parking spots in real-time, reducing the stress of finding parking in busy areas. With features like location-based search, slot booking, and navigation assistance, PickSpot provides a hassle-free experience from booking to parking. The interface was crafted with simplicity and efficiency in mind, ensuring a smooth and user-friendly journey that helps users save time and improve their overall parking experience.

MediCare!

Medicare – Healthcare Management App

The Medicare app is designed as a comprehensive healthcare solution, empowering patients to access their medical history, book appointments, schedule tests, and order medications all in one place. With over 20 meticulously crafted screens on Figma, this app offers an intuitive and user-centric experience that prioritizes patient convenience and accessibility. By streamlining essential health services, Medicare aims to make managing personal healthcare straightforward and efficient. The design approach emphasizes clarity and ease-of-use, ensuring users can seamlessly navigate their health journey with confidence.
